Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Barbellz ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Barbellz collects only the data you directly provide:

  • Workout data — exercises, sets, weights, reps, and session history
  • Nutrition data — meals, food items, calorie and macro logs
  • Body metrics — weight logs you enter manually
  • Profile information — your name, age, height, weight goal (used to calculate calorie targets)
  • Account information — email address if you sign in with Apple

2. How We Use Your Data

All data you enter is used solely to provide the Barbellz app experience:

  • Displaying your workout history, progress charts, and personal records
  • Calculating your daily nutrition goals and macro tracking
  • Powering the AI Coach feature (your nutrition and workout context is sent to our AI service to generate responses)
  • Storing your preferences and settings

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your workout and nutrition data is stored locally on your device using encrypted on-device storage. If you use Sign in with Apple, your account is managed through Apple's authentication service.

Food search uses a cloud database (Supabase) to look up nutritional information — search queries are sent to retrieve food data but are not linked to your identity.

4. AI Coach

The AI Coach feature sends anonymized context (such as today's calorie total, macros, and days since last workout) to a third-party AI provider (Google Gemini) to generate coaching responses. No personally identifiable information is included in these requests.

5. Subscription & Payments

Subscription management is handled entirely by Apple's App Store and RevenueCat. We do not store or process payment information. Your purchase history is managed by Apple.

6. Camera & Photo Access

Barbellz requests camera access only to scan barcodes and nutrition labels. Photos are not uploaded to any server and are processed locally on your device.

7. Children's Privacy

Barbellz is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13 years of age.

8. Your Rights

You can delete all your data at any time by going to Profile → Delete All Data within the app, or by uninstalling the app. Since data is stored locally, uninstalling the app removes all associated data from your device.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
